ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Ivan Jade G. Lugo. I live in San Vicente, City of Ilagan, Isabela. I am 19 years old and I was born on October 20, 2002. I am an only child. I grew up with my Grandmother because my parents worked abroad. I studied elementary at Ilagan East Central School and I joined drum and lyre. I completed grade 7 to grade 12 at Isabela National High School. When I was 15 years old my mother returned to the Philippines. She is now a massage therapist and also sells snacks at the Xentro Mall. My Father is still working in Hong Kong so that he can provide for my needs and my education. My parents are not together but they are on good terms.

I am currently studying at Isabela State University and the course I am taking is Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. My first 2 years in college were challenging because It was conducted online due to the COVID-19 virus. Because face-to-face is near, I'm happy because I can focus more especially on my studies and also having a new circle of friends.
